P(X=xi∣Y=yj)=P(X=xi,Y=yj)P(Y=yj)=Pijp.j(i=1,2,...)P(X = x_i | Y = y_j) = \frac{P(X = x_i, Y = y_j)}{P(Y = y_j)} = \frac{P_{ij}}{p.j} \quad (i=1,2,...)P(X=xi​∣Y=yj​)=P(Y=yj​)P(X=xi​,Y=yj​)​=p.jPij​​(i=1,2,...)

连续随机变量的条件分布

二元连续型随机变量(X,Y)(X, Y)(X,Y)的联合密度函数为f(x,y)f(x, y)f(x,y)，对一切使YYY的边缘密度函数fY(y)>0f_Y(y) > 0fY​(y)>0的yyy，给定Y=yY = yY=y条件下XXX的条件分布函数为：

F(x∣y)=P(X⩽x∣Y=y)F(x|y) = P(X \leqslant x | Y = y)F(x∣y)=P(X⩽x∣Y=y)

=lim⁡h→0P(X⩽x∣y⩽Y⩽y+h)\quad\quad\quad\quad=\lim\limits_{h\rightarrow0}P(X \leqslant x | y \leqslant Y \leqslant y + h)=h→0lim​P(X⩽x∣y⩽Y⩽y+h)

=lim⁡h→0P(X⩽x,y⩽Y⩽y+h)P(y⩽Y⩽y+h)\quad\quad\quad\quad=\lim\limits_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{P(X \leqslant x, y \leqslant Y \leqslant y + h)}{P(y \leqslant Y \leqslant y + h)}=h→0lim​P(y⩽Y⩽y+h)P(X⩽x,y⩽Y⩽y+h)​

=∫−∞xf(u,y)fY(y)du\quad\quad\quad\quad=\int_{-\infty}^x \frac{f(u, y)}{f_Y(y)}du=∫−∞x​fY​(y)f(u,y)​du
